Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Weather app, developed and published by CarebridgePro.

EFFECTIVE DATE · JUNE 20, 2026

1. Who We Are

Weather app is developed by CarebridgePro ("we," "us," or "our"), based in the United States. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you as a user.

2. Information We Collect

Location data. With your permission, Weather app accesses your device's GPS location in order to display current conditions and forecasts for your area. Location data is used only to retrieve weather information and is not sold or shared with advertisers.

Saved locations and searches. Locations you add or search for are stored locally on your device in a local database. This information is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Notifications. If enabled, the app sends local weather-related notifications (such as daily forecasts or condition alerts) generated directly on your device.

Weather app does not collect device identifiers, does not use analytics or tracking SDKs, and does not display advertising or use advertising identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide weather data, the app sends your location coordinates to WeatherAPI (weatherapi.com), a third-party weather data provider, solely to retrieve current conditions and forecasts. This request is made directly from your device and is not logged or stored by us.

We encourage you to review WeatherAPI's own Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data sent to their service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Saved locations and app preferences are stored locally on your device using a local database. Since this data does not leave your device under normal operation, you remain in control of it at all times. You can remove this data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

5. Your Choices

  • You can grant or revoke location permission at any time from your device's system settings.
  • You can disable notifications at any time from your device's system settings.
  • You can delete locally stored data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Weather app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable law. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.
